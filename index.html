ABOUT GENESIS

What is Genesis?

Genesis Hackathon is an inter-school innovation event that brings together the brightest young minds to solve real-world problems through technology and creativity.

Hosted by SIN Innovation at St. Austin's Sr. Sec. School on October 25, 2025, this event aims to foster innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving skills among students.

Our Mission

To create a platform where students can transform their ideas into reality, developing solutions that address challenges in our community and beyond.

We believe in nurturing the next generation of innovators who will lead us into a new era of technological advancement and social impact.

EVENT RULES

Team Formation

Minimum 1 and maximum 4 members per team.
Only 25 teams will be selected to participate.

Discipline & Conduct

Maintain proper discipline at all times.
No fighting or misbehavior — violation will lead to immediate disqualification.

Reporting Time

Reporting time is 8:30 AM and is compulsory for all participants.
Punctuality is mandatory; latecomers will face appropriate disciplinary action.

Event Structure

The event will include 2 mentoring rounds and 2 judging rounds.
Attendance in all rounds is mandatory.

Judging & Fair Play

Judges' decisions are final and binding.
Teams must maintain originality, honesty, and fair play throughout the event.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

💀 1. What is a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is an event where participants come together to solve problems, innovate, and build projects within a limited time. It encourages teamwork, creativity, and practical application of skills, often in technology, AI, or real-world challenges.

🧠 2. What is Genesis Hackathon 2025?

Genesis Hackathon 2025 is a one-day innovation event organized by St. Austin's Sen. Sec. School in collaboration with SIN Technologies. It encourages students to create smart, AI-driven, and impactful solutions for real-world problems.

📅 3. When and where will the event take place?

📍 The event will be held on 25th October 2025, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM at St. Austin's Sen. Sec. School, Jodhpur.

👥 4. Who can participate?

Students from Classes 6th to 12th can participate in this exciting hackathon and showcase their innovative skills.

👨‍💻 5. Can I participate alone or do I need a team?

You can participate solo or in a team of 1 to 4 members. Collaboration is encouraged to combine different skills and ideas.

🧩 6. What are the idea themes?

Participants can choose from the following themes:

  • Young Bharat Builders (Local & Rural Innovation)
  • Smart Mobility for All
  • Health at Home
  • Make for the Planet
💰 7. Are there any prizes?

Yes! Exciting prizes, trophies, and certificates await the top teams.

  • 🥇 1st Prize: Certificate + Trophy
  • 🥈 2nd Prize: Certificate + Trophy
  • 🥉 3rd Prize: Certificate + Trophy

🎖️ All participants will receive E-certificates and goodies.

📝 8. How can I register?

Click the "Register Now" button on the website or fill out the Google Form before 25th October 2025, 8:00 AM.

🧾 9. What should I bring on the event day?

Bring your student ID card, laptop (if needed), and presentation materials (slides, posters, or prototypes).

💡 10. What kind of ideas are expected?

Ideas should be innovative, practical, and impactful — solving real-world challenges through technology or creativity. Prototype or conceptual presentations are both welcome.

📜 11. Will participants receive certificates?

Yes! All participants will receive E-certificates, and winners will get printed certificates and trophies.

☕ 12. Will refreshments be provided?

Yes! Snacks and lunch will be provided during the event.
